Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a classic Italian-American dish featuring a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and spicy, savory pepperoni slices.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) of active dry yeast
  • 1 cup of warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Toppings:

  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20-25 slices of pepperoni
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms. Knead on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until smooth. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for about 1 hour until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, preheat it as well.
  3. Shape the Dough: Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into a 12-inch circle. Place it on a pizza pan or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Add the Toppings: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top. Arrange the pepperoni slices evenly. Optionally, sprinkle oregano and red pepper flakes for extra flavor.
  5. Bake the Pizza: Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Serve and Enjoy: Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice and enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
